# Product Scorecard

Evaluate released games and major product changes after evidence is available.

Score 1–5:

- Fun within five seconds.
- Replay motivation.
- Share motivation.
- Friend competition value.
- Mobile clarity and performance.
- Accessibility and reduced motion.
- Arabic RTL, English, and Turkish quality.
- Islamic-content confidence.
- Originality.
- Visual quality.
- Motion and game feel.
- Maintenance cost.
- 2D/3D strategic value.

## Decision

Product Health recommends exactly one:

- improve;
- expand;
- experiment;
- retain;
- retire;
- move to the next concept.

Do not select by total score alone. Record evidence, risks, and the next visible delivery.
