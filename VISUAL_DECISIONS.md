# Visual Decisions

This file prevents repeated visual directions and records only decisions that affect future challenge selection. Keep entries concise and factual.

## Platform shell

- Font Awesome is used for platform interface icons.
- Arabic RTL, English, and Turkish share one coherent shell.
- Platform controls, cards, result, comparison, sharing, audio/mute, and accessibility patterns remain consistent across challenges.
- Challenge art may vary substantially, but must integrate cleanly with the platform shell.

## Visual direction rules

- Do not use the same art family for two consecutive challenge deliveries.
- Do not repeat the same camera, control style, puzzle structure, pressure rhythm, or progression model in consecutive challenges unless a documented product reason outweighs the repetition risk.
- Each zone must differ through composition, geometry, lighting, hazards, or environment behavior—not only a background color swap.
- Player, hazard, safe, interactive, and puzzle silhouettes must remain distinguishable without relying on color alone.
- Strong genre-level inspiration is allowed; exact characters, brands, assets, textures, levels, UI, sounds, signature animations, trade dress, and recognizable compositions are not.
