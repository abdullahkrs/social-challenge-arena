# Pulsebound Vertical Slice

Issue: #119

## Core
Pulsebound is an endless auto-running platform challenge controlled with one finger: tap to jump, swipe up to switch to the upper route, swipe down to slide and return low. Seeded routes combine movement hazards with symbol gates that require matching, inversion, counting, memory and transformation.

## Visible inspiration transformation
- Sonic: momentum, layered parallax and flowing terrain rhythm become a two-route world with moving scenery and fast recovery.
- Super Mario: readable player silhouette, jump anticipation and obstacle grammar become simple body poses and strongly shaped hazards.
- Pepsiman: forward pressure and staged surprises become dramatic event entry from the right edge.
- Flappy Bird: immediate touch response and instant retry rhythm remain, without copying the one-button loop.
- Minecraft: modular biome construction becomes block-like platform materials and seven procedural worlds.

No characters, brands, exact levels, palettes, UI, sounds, textures, poses, animations or recognizable compositions are copied.

## First-cycle progression
1. Learn: tap-jump and single hazards.
2. Apply: upper/lower route switching.
3. Combine: jump or slide while selecting a route.
4. Deceive: inverse and memory gates.
5. Pressure: stacked hazards and shorter decisions.
6. Mastery: mixed gates, route changes and precision.
7. Recovery: lower intensity and chance restoration.

## Mobile gate
The arena uses dynamic viewport height, safe-area insets and touch-action none. The production matrix covers 360x640, 390x844, 412x915 and 844x390 in Arabic, English and Turkish.
