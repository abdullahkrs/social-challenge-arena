# Creative Brief

## Desired feeling

Fast, playful, tense, colorful, surprising, responsive, and immediately understandable.

## Product personality

Modern mobile clarity with original arcade energy and selective nostalgia from the 1990s and 2000s.

## Target audience

Casual mobile players who want to challenge friends without registration.

## Session length

Most rounds should last 15–60 seconds.

## Every concept must answer

- Why is this fun in the first five seconds?
- What makes the player want one more try?
- Why would a friend accept the challenge?
- What creates a memorable visual moment?
- What makes the score feel fair and shareable?
- How does the concept remain understandable in English and Arabic?

## Creative priorities

1. Strong game feel before catalog size.
2. One clear input with meaningful consequences.
3. Visible player agency and animated world interaction.
4. Escalating tension and dramatic near misses.
5. Original visual identity.
6. Social hooks that encourage rematch and share-again.
7. Short replayable sessions.
8. Accessible reduced-motion equivalents.

## Avoid

- Static button tests.
- Cosmetic reskins.
- Long tutorials.
- Copied characters, assets, levels, names, sounds, or trade dress.
- Fake popularity or rankings.
- Dark patterns.
- Features requiring login before core value.
- Ideas that depend on one social platform.
