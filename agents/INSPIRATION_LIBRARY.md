# Inspiration Library

Use this library to explore generic mechanics and emotions without copying existing games.

## Mechanic inspiration

- One-touch vertical movement.
- Gravity and momentum.
- Aim and release.
- Bounce and ricochet.
- Lane switching.
- Risk-reward collection.
- Rhythm windows.
- Moving-target precision.
- Chain reactions.
- Environmental timing.
- Short platform survival.
- Dynamic obstacle patterns.
- Balance and stacking.
- Destruction and breakable targets.

## Emotional inspiration

- Near miss.
- Comeback.
- Last-second success.
- Surprise transformation.
- Increasing tension.
- Perfect timing.
- Destruction satisfaction.
- Combo momentum.
- Friendly revenge.
- One-more-try frustration relief.

## Social inspiration

- Rematch.
- Best-of-three.
- Revenge challenge.
- Score-gap challenge.
- Challenge chain.
- Asynchronous team total.
- Attempt replay.
- Friend-generated modifier.
- Risk handicap.

## Rule

A new concept must differ materially from existing work in input, movement, failure, scoring, or social interaction. Changing only theme, duration, speed, or visuals is not a new concept.
