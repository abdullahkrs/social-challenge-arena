# Metrics

The MVP must be designed around the viral loop, even before full analytics are implemented.

## Funnel Metrics

1. Challenge link opens.
2. Challenge starts.
3. Challenge completions.
4. Result views.
5. Share button clicks.
6. Copy link clicks.
7. Friend attempts from shared links.
8. Comparison views.
9. Repeat shares after comparison.

## Conversion Rates

- Open → Start.
- Start → Complete.
- Complete → Share.
- Share → Friend Open.
- Friend Open → Friend Complete.
- Compare → Share Again.

## Activation Signal

A user is activated when they complete a challenge and share the result or challenge link.

## Viral Signal

A viral loop is working when one completed challenge produces at least one friend attempt often enough to justify further template development.

## MVP Success Thresholds — Initial Targets

These are starting assumptions, not final targets:

- 40% of link openers start the challenge.
- 60% of starters complete it.
- 20% of completers use share/copy.
- 25% of shared-link openers attempt the challenge.
- 10% of comparison viewers share again.

## Metrics Deferred

- Revenue.
- Paid conversion.
- Creator retention.
- Long-term cohort retention.
- Advanced attribution.

These are not MVP blockers.
