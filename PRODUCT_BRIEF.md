# Product Brief

## Vision

Build a continuously evolving social arcade platform for broad social-media audiences. The platform supports polished 2D, 2.5D, and 3D challenges, strong graphics and animation, multilingual use, asynchronous friend competition, and continuous product improvement.

## Core loop

Discover → understand → play → finish or fail → result → replay → share → friend competes → compare → share again.

## Platform scope

The platform includes discovery, catalog, game hosting, loading, result handling, replay, strict share links, friend challenge, comparison, localization, accessibility, privacy-safe metrics, performance management, and deployment.

## Challenge scope

Challenges may use original 2D, 2.5D, or 3D mechanics. Dimension is selected by value, clarity, performance, accessibility, originality, and maintenance—not by quota.

## Required languages

Arabic with full RTL, English, and Turkish from the first playable delivery. More languages may be added when justified.

## Islamic constraints

All content must comply with `ISLAMIC_CONTENT_POLICY.md`. Unclear or questionable elements are removed, replaced with neutral alternatives, or the concept is rejected. Work never waits for owner decisions.

## Product balance

After every visible delivery, the swarm must determine the largest current constraint:

- platform experience;
- challenge quality or variety;
- social loop;
- localization or accessibility;
- performance or reliability.

The next delivery addresses exactly one highest-value constraint, while proving shared platform capabilities through a real user journey.

## Non-goals until explicitly proven necessary

Accounts, public feeds, public user-generated content, real-time multiplayer, gambling, invasive data collection, manipulative monetization, and generic game-engine construction.
