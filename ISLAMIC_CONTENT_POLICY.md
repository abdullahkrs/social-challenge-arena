# Islamic Content Policy

This policy applies to concepts, visuals, animation, audio, text, rewards, monetization, social mechanics, trends, marketing, and releases.

## Prohibited

- Nudity, sexualization, indecency, suggestive presentation, or romanticized immoral conduct.
- Gambling, betting, loot-box gambling, or monetary chance-based rewards.
- Alcohol, drugs, smoking, or prohibited-substance promotion.
- Religious mockery, occult ritual, devotional polytheistic symbolism, or worship simulation.
- Vulgarity, humiliation, bullying, abusive pressure, unsafe physical challenges, or harmful imitation.
- Deceptive monetization, child exploitation, manipulative dark patterns, or privacy invasion.
- Content that attacks religions, peoples, cultures, families, or protected groups.

## Required review

Every concept and release candidate must record:

- content themes;
- character and clothing treatment;
- symbols and environments;
- audio approach;
- reward and monetization mechanics;
- social pressure and safety risks;
- regional or cultural sensitivities;
- review result: PASS / REVISE / ESCALATE.

## Uncertainty

When a question depends on an unclear religious ruling, agents must use `ESCALATE` and request owner guidance. They must not guess, issue a fatwa, or silently weaken the policy.
