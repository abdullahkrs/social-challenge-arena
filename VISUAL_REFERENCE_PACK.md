# Rift Relay Visual Reference Pack

Issue: #117

## Named gameplay references

The rebuild studies public gameplay from the following named references at a genre level:

- **Sonic the Hedgehog** — momentum, readable slopes, layered parallax, speed communicated through world motion.
- **Super Mario** — clear player silhouette, obstacle grammar, anticipation before jumps, readable safe/danger states.
- **Pepsiman** — forward-runner camera, dramatic obstacle staging, rapid route reading, comic impact feedback.
- **Flappy Bird** — immediate touch response, one-action clarity, unforgiving precision, instant restart rhythm.
- **Minecraft** — modular world construction, distinct biomes, block-based depth, strong material silhouettes.

## Inspiration matrix

| Area | Reference quality | Original transformation in Rift Relay |
|---|---|---|
| Movement | Sonic momentum + Flappy immediacy | Swipe commits the carrier between curved energy rails; tap creates a short pulse-jump with a visible arc |
| Camera | Pepsiman forward chase | Low chase camera with converging rails, horizon reveals and staged obstacle silhouettes |
| World construction | Minecraft modular biomes | Procedurally assembled floating relay ruins, signal bridges, vault chambers and collapsing foundries |
| Player silhouette | Mario readability | Original faceted signal courier with bright core, dark outline and readable airborne pose |
| Obstacles | Mario anticipation + Pepsiman staging | Distinct families: gates, gaps, sweepers, crushers, rotating prisms and false beacons |
| Puzzle telegraphing | Mario visual grammar | Shape, number, direction and pulse-order symbols are introduced before they are combined |
| Color hierarchy | Sonic foreground/background separation | Safe cyan, danger coral, active gold and neutral stone, reinforced by shape and motion |
| Feedback | Flappy restart clarity + Sonic impact | screen pulse, trail break, signal shards, brief freeze and immediate recovery/restart |
| Pacing | Sonic flow + Pepsiman set pieces | understanding, application, combination, deception, pressure, mastery, then recovery |
| Touch feel | Flappy directness | no permanent gameplay button row; swipe lanes, tap or swipe-up to jump |

## Moodboard observations

Review 4–8 public gameplay screenshots and 2–3 gameplay videos from the named titles before changing camera, silhouette, obstacle scale or biome composition. Reference media is not committed as product assets.

## Originality boundary

The implementation does not copy characters, brands, items, levels, exact palettes, UI, textures, sounds, music, signature poses, signature animations, trade dress or recognizable compositions. It combines high-level qualities into an original signal-relay world, original rules and original procedural layouts.
