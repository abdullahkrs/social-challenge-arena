# Roadmap

## MVP Goal

Prove that a mobile user can open a social challenge, play or create it quickly, get a result, share it, and make a friend compete through one link.

## Cycle 1 Product Choice

Chosen direction: one-link friend challenge templates.

Rejected for MVP:

- Full game portal.
- Many challenge categories.
- Accounts and profiles.
- Paid creator tools.
- Real-time multiplayer.

## Product Direction Alternatives Reviewed

| Direction | Understandability | Shareability | Friend Competition | Virality | MVP Ease | Monetization | Extensibility | Decision |
|---|---:|---:|---:|---:|---:|---:|---:|---|
| One-link friend challenge templates | 5 | 5 | 5 | 5 | 5 | 4 | 5 | Start here |
| Who knows me best? challenge | 5 | 5 | 4 | 5 | 4 | 4 | 4 | Future template |
| Creator audience challenge | 4 | 5 | 3 | 5 | 3 | 5 | 4 | Later |
| Daily public challenge | 4 | 4 | 4 | 4 | 4 | 3 | 4 | Later |
| Voting / this-or-that challenge | 5 | 4 | 3 | 4 | 5 | 3 | 5 | Later |

Scale: 1 weak, 5 strong.

## MVP Phases

### Phase 1 — Product shell

- Mobile-first landing page. ✅
- Explain the challenge in one screen. ✅
- CTA: Play a sample challenge. ✅

### Phase 2 — First playable challenge

- Single challenge template. ✅
- Local scoring. ✅
- Result screen. ✅

### Phase 3 — Sharing loop

- Share/copy result link. ✅
- Friend opens same challenge.
- Friend attempt is recorded.

### Phase 4 — Comparison loop

- Compare original-player and friend score.
- Prompt loser/winner to share again.

### Phase 5 — MVP validation

- Basic event metrics.
- Manual review of conversion rates.
- Decide whether to add more templates.

## Definition of MVP Complete

MVP is complete only when:

- A new user can play from mobile without login.
- An original player can share a challenge link.
- A friend can open the link and compete.
- Both users can see a comparison.
- The result page encourages another share.
- Basic metrics can track open, start, complete, share, and friend attempt.

## Next Suggested Task

Open a shared link with the original score preserved and let the friend start an independent attempt, without implementing comparison yet.
