## Admission rules

A game may enter `Prototype` only when it has:

- an original concept contract;
- a complete vertical-slice issue;
- a defined visual brief and motion brief;
- a bounded score and explicit failure state;
- a clear friend-versus-friend social hook;
- a mobile performance plan;
- reduced-motion behavior;
- an originality review.

A game may enter `Active` only after it is reachable from discovery, playable end to end, QA-passed, share-compatible, preview-verified, and recorded in `PRODUCT_SCORECARD.md`.

## Mechanical diversity gate

A new game must differ from active games in at least two of:

- input;
- movement;
- failure;
- scoring;
- pressure pattern;
- social interaction.

Theme, duration, speed, particles, or art style alone do not create a new game.

## Retirement rules

Move a game toward retirement when evidence shows weak replay, weak sharing, unclear controls, poor mobile performance, duplicated mechanics, high maintenance cost, or a better replacement. Preserve valid shared-link compatibility only when explicitly required.
