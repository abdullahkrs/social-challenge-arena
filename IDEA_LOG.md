# Idea Log

Track creative concepts without turning every brainstorm into roadmap work.

## Status values

- Proposed
- Developing
- Experiment
- Approved
- Parked
- Rejected
- Implemented

## Ideas

| ID | Idea | Status | Owner | Decision reason | Related trend/experiment |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|

## Rules

- Use IDs `I-YYYY-NNN`.
- Record one concise row per concept.
- Put detailed concept work in a dedicated issue, `BACKLOG.md`, or `EXPERIMENTS.md`.
- Never mark an idea Approved without Coordinator or owner approval.
- Never mark an idea Validated without evidence.
- Compare new ideas against legacy mechanics, existing ideas, rejected ideas, and recent creative cycles.
