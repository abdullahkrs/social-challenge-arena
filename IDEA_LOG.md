# Idea Log

Track proposed, developing, approved, parked, rejected, implemented, and retired ideas.

| ID | Idea | Status | Product problem | Dimension | Social hook | Islamic review | Languages | Decision reason |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|

## Rules

- Use IDs `I-YYYY-NNN`.
- Compare each idea with existing catalog mechanics and rejected ideas.
- A reskin is not a new idea.
- No idea becomes implementation work before Product Strategy, Islamic Content Review, Visual Direction, and Motion Design are ready.
